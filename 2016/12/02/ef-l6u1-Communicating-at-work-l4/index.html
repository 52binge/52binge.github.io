Communicating at work L4 Summarizing a meeting   2016-12-02


1. Vocabulary

  • agenda item
  • new hire
  • personal reasons

Dialog
[TODD] Okay. Next item on the agenda is the company update. Some of you have asked me about Sunset Computers.
[HARRY] Yeah. What’s going on, Todd?
[TODD] Yesterday, I asked Joan what was happening. She confirmed that we are buying Sunset Computers.
[HARRY] Wow! That’s big news!
[HARRY] So, what is that going to mean for us?
[TODD] It’s very good news. You know we’ve been having trouble with sales in Asia, right?
[TODD] Well, this deal could make us stronger over there. Sunset Computers have big customers in China and Korea. [kə’rɪə]
[SALLY] Oh, yeah. That’s right
[HARRY] But do we need to worry about our jobs?
[TODD] Absolutely not. The outlook is really good. Oh, and one more thing. This is confidential. Do not talk to anyone outside the company.
[TODD] Great. Are there any more questions?
[TODD] So, to summarize: This deal is good for us. No job cuts. The outlook is great. And there’s only one action item - please clean up your desks.
[TODD] Okay, that’s it. Thank you for coming.
[HARRY] Did you know anything about this?
[SALLY] I heard some people talking about it, but …

2. Expressions

told/said that time
Cody says he wants to know the latest sales figures present
Cody said he wants to know the latest sales figures past
How’s the team doing? Cody asked how the team is doing. 团队工作表现怎么样？
Where are you meeting? Cody asked where we are meeting.
Who are the visitors? Cody asked who the visitors are.
Why are the visitors coming? Cody asked why the visitors are coming.

3. Anyone / Anything

Anyone Anything
Does anyone have questions? I don’t have anything to report.
Don’t tell anyone about this. Mike, do you want to say anything?
How about the visitors from Turkey? Is anyone here yet? Does anyone have anything else to say?
Don’t tell anyone about the update Do you want anything from the snack table?
Is anyone taking notes?

4. Writing

  • I’m writing to summarize our meeting about the Royal project.
  • Randy asked who is working on the project.
  • Jeremy said that Bill and Lucy are working on the project
  • Jeremy said that he can confirm the budget by Monday.

Summary of Friday’s sales meeting

Summary of Monday’s sales meeting

5. Some sentence

agenda
Good morning. Glad you’re all here.
Has everyone looked at the agenda?
There are seven items on the agenda.
We don’t have much time
Has everyone seen the agenda?
Now let’s move to item two - company updates.
