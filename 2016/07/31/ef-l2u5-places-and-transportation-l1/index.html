Places and transportation L1 Deciding where to go on the weekend   2016-07-31


Place Word

  • the swimming pool
  • train station
  • restaurant
  • museum

Main Dialog
A : Why don’t we go to the swimming pool?
B : I donot like swimming
A : How about the library?
B : library~
A : I know, Let’s go to the park.
B : Yeah, good idea.

Expressions Sentence

  • How about watching a movie?
  • What about a game of cards?
  • Why don’t we go to a restaurant?
  • Why don’t we go to the swimming pool?
  • Why don’t we go to the gym?
  • Let’s go swimming.
  • Thanks, but I can’t swim.
  • What about the library?
  • Let’s go to the museum.
     
  • Let’s get something to eat.
  • The library is next to the park

Agreeing or saying yes : Huh? or Uh-huh. or Yeah. or Yup.
Disagreeing or saying no : Nah. or Nope. or Uh-uh.

Man Woman
Do you like playing golf? Nope.
But you like swimming, right? Yeah
OK, let’s go to the beach. Huh?
You like the beach, right? Yup
Or we can play golf Nah. . Let’s go to the beach.

  • She’s going to the bank?
  • You don’t like French food?
  • They’re at the train station?
