1. Calling in sick

  • ABC Industries. Mary speaking / This is Mary.

Main Dialog
A : Calling~~~
B : Todd Billings.
A : Hi, Todd. This is Sally.
B : Hi, Sally. hey, What’s up? Where are you?
A : I’m at home.
B : What’s wrong?
A : I’m not feeling well/bad/better，I’ve got a cold.
B : sounds bad !
A : I’m sorry, I cann’t come to work today
B : OK, You should stay your home
A : Sorry about work.
B : No problem!
A : Thank you

2. Automated voicemail

Sometimes you will get automated voicemail when you call a business. You might hear:

  1. Please leave a message after the tone.
  2. We are currently closed. Please call again.

3. Common as greetings

These expressions are common as greetings.

  • what’s up? = What’s happening? = What’s going on?

  • What’s up with you? = What’s going on with you?

4. Negative situation

Man : BILL Woman : Sarah
Hey, Sarah. What's wrong ? Nothing, Bill. Don't worry about it.
Really? What's the matter? Well, my sister is very sick .
Oh, I’m so sorry. What's the problem ? The doctors don’t know .

5. Calling Talk

A : ABC Industries. How can I help you?
B : This is Bill. Can I speak to Mary?
A : Just a minute, please.

  • Sorry about work.
  • Sorry about your car. (you damaged your friend’s car.)
  • Sorry about the meeting. (you missed a meeting.)
