Verbs and gerunds

After the verbs like, love, hate and enjoy, you can use a gerund.

pharse sentence
like + gerund I like playing basketball
love + gerund She loves going to the beach
hate + gerund He hates doing yaga
running、riding a bike、painting playing soccer、cooking、dancing
I love playing basketball Do you like playing basketball
