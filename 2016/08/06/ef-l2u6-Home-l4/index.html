Home L4 Describing your home   2016-08-06


1. Vocabulary

  • lovely、modern、clean、quiet、safe
  • ugly、old、dirty、noisy、dangerous

Main Dialog
M : How's the new apartment?
S : It’s great. I like it.
M : Tell me about it. Is the neighborhood safe?
S : Yes, It’s very safe.
M : What’s the room like？
S : It’s prtty big. I like it.
M : Ok. What about the rest of the apartment?
S : It’s nice, I share the kitchen room, living room and bathroom with my roommate
M : You share the bathroom? Is it clean?
S : It’s very clean.
M : What’s the kitchen like?
S : It’s good. That’s fridge and stove、dishwasher.
M : It’s very nice.

2. Expressions

  • Tell me about your apartment.
  • There’s a balcony and a modern kitchen.
  • You have six roommates?
  • Wow! That sounds uncomfortable.

Adjectives for describing a home

  • Her home is really lovely.
  • John lives in an ugly house.
     
  • His apartment is always clean.
  • I can’t live in a dirty house.
     
  • I prefer modern buildings.
  • His house is very old.
     
  • It’s a safe city to live in.
  • I live in a dangerous neighborhood.
     
  • New York City is so noisy!
  • My street is very quiet.

3. Reading

  • Our apartment is very nice, but there are some problems.
  • Our place is quiet, but the neighborhood is dangerous sometimes.
  • Our kitchen is modern and lovely, but the dishwasher is noisy.
  • The bedroom is small, and the bathroom’s a little ugly.
  • The living room is great. We have a big TV and really comfortable chairs.
