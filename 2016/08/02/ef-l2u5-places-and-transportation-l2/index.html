Places and transportation L2 Getting around town   2016-08-02


Asking for a location

  • Where’s Cafe Delight ?
  • It’s on 5th Street, near the park.
  • Do you know where Central Park is?
  • Pardon me. Do you know where Central Park is?

Main Dialog
A: Excuse me. Do you know where the Central Park is?
B: Oh，yes. It’s on Green Street.
A: Is it near the train station?
B: No，it isn’t. It’s near the museum.
A: How do I get there?
B: First，take the bus to the subway station. Second，take the subway to the park. Third，walk to the museum.
A: It’s complex
B: You can also only take the number 12 bus. and get off at the seven stop，then walk to the museum
A: How long does it take by 12 bus?
B: It takes about 55 minutes.

Giving transportation advice

  • How do I get to the museum?
  • How do I get there?
     
  • You should go by bus.
  • You can get to the park by subway.
  • You can get there by subway.
     
  • Take the bus. It’s raining.

  • You can take the train to the beach.
     

  • Take the number 17 bus to the subway station.

  • You’re going there by subway? Take the blue line.
     
  • Get on Line 4.
  • Change at the university to Line 2.
