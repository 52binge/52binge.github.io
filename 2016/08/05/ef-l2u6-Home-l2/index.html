Finding an apartment

  • apartments for rent
  • near public transportation
  • Apartment comes furnished and has large bathrooms.
  • 1,100 = one thousand, one hundred; or eleven hundred
  • The apartment is thirteen hundred.

 

Main Dialog
A : I’m looking at apartments for rent.
B : Anything near public transportation ?
A : Well, here’s one. Seventeen hundred dollars a month.
B : Wow. That’s not cheap !
A : No, but it comes furnished. [‘fɜːnɪʃt]
B : How big is it?
A : Two bed, one bath. And pets are okay.
