Home L1 Talking about your home   2016-08-04


Vocabulary

  • apartment，yard
  • bedroom，dining room
  • bathroom，living room
  • balcony，kitchen，garage [ˈgærɑ:ʒ]

A: Whose beautiful yard is that?
B: It’s ours.

Main Dialog
A : So, Li, this is your family’s new house?
B : Welcome, Peter. This is our living room.
A : Nice. And you have three bedrooms?
B : Yes, this bedroom is my husband’s and mine
A : Okay. And this blue bedroom，is this your son’s bedroom?
B : Yes, that’s Billy’s bedroom.
A : Whose bedroom is this?
B : Oh, my husband’s parents are here for two weeks. That bedroom is theirs

Vocabulary

  • closet，sofa
  • shower，dishwasher，stove
  • sink，refrigerator [riˈfridʒəreitə]
Main Dialog
A : Hey, Eric, I have a new house!
B : That’s great, Mona! Where is it?
A : It’s on Green Street, next to the park and the United Bank.
B : Sounds good. What’s it like ?
A : Well, it has one bedroom and one bathroom.
B : Oh, is it too small for you?
A : Not really, but it’s expensive.
B : Too bad.
A : That’s okay. My husband’s parents are helping us
B : That’s nice. Do you like the house?
A : Yeah, it’s beautiful! The living room’s painted yellow and has three big windows. And the bedroom has a balcony.
