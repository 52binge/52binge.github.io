Places and transportation L3 Describing your neighborhood   2016-08-03


Vocabulary

  • playground，coffee shop，pizza place，bookstore
  • bakery [‘beɪk(ə)rɪ] k/g，movie theater [‘θɪətə]
  • parking lot，bus stop

Main Dialog
A : Do you know a good place for lunch?
B : there’s a good pizza place.
A : Uh-huh?
B : Oh, and two thai restaurant
A : I like thai，Which one do you like?
B : They’re both good. but the one across the street is excellent！
A : Great, Where’s that
B : on the corner Pine Street，next to movie theater
A : That‘s a little far，12 bus？
B : Yes，take the 12 bus. and get off at senond shop
A : Great！

Locations in a city

  • The library is next to the playground.
  • The bank is on the corner of Pine Street and First Street.
  • The bus stop is in front of the supermarket.
     
  • The post office is between the restaurant and the movie theater.
  • The pizza place is behind the library.
     
  • The bookstore is across the street.
  • The bookstore is across the street from the restaurant.

Asking for and making recommendations

  • Do you know a good place for coffee?
  • Do you know a nice park near here?
     
  • There’s a good place for coffee on the corner.
  • There’s a nice park on Pine Street, next to the bank.
  • There are two Thai restaurants near here.
     
  • Which restaurant do you like?
  • They’re both very good, but the one across the street is expensive.
  • They’re both good, but that one is too expensive.

Reading

I live in a great neighborhood. The people are friendly, and there are many good shops. There’s a delicious bakery across the street. There’s a nice coffee shop on the corner. Sometimes I eat at the Italian restaurant next to the bakery. The food is good, but it’s a little expensive. My favorite shop is the bookstore. It’s near the park.

Writing

My neighborhood is big and busy. There’s a thai restaurant across the street. There is a library and a big department store. There are a lot of good shops and restaurants. There’s a coffee shop and a good pizza place. My favorite place is the park. It’s on the corner of Elm Street and First Street. I go there every weekend.
