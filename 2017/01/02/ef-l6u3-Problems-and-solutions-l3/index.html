Problems and solutions L3 Solving an employee problem   2017-01-02


1. Vocabulary

  • being late = tardiness
  • being sloppy
  • being argumentative
  • being offensive
  • not being flexible
  • not following instructions
  • not working well with the team
  • not meeting deadlines
  • emphasize

A: HARRY - B: TODD
[TODD] You see, it’s becoming a bit of a problem. Sometimes you’re as much as an hour late.
[HARRY] I’m really sorry. I’ve been meaning to talk to you about this.
[TODD] So, what’s the story ?
[HARRY] And it’s now me who drives the kids to school every morning. That’s what’s making me late.
[TODD] I know how that is. I have two kids of my own. But regardless, we have to figure out a solution to this.
[HARRY] I agree.
[TODD] Another possibility would be for you to stay an hour later in the evenings.
[HARRY] Yeah, that would work. That would definitely work. Thanks, Todd. I appreciate your flexibility on this.
[TODD] Let’s try this for a couple of weeks, and see how it goes.
[HARRY] OKay !
[TODD] And I want to emphasize [‘emfəsaɪz] that we value your work. This isn’t a big deal.

2. Expressions

A: JOSH - B: PAM
JOSH: What did you think of this activity? My boss wanted to speak with me today.
PAM: What for ?
JOSH: For not meeting my deadline, and also for being argumentative.
PAM: Did your boss mention anything else?
JOSH: He said I need to work on being more flexible.
PAM: Yeah … Good luck with that.
sentence
We have a lot of engineers who work late.
I work with a guy. He never meets deadlines.
We have to figure out a solution for this.
One solution could be that I come early.
Another possibility would be for me to work late.
She was warned about not being flexible enough.
Sorry! I wasn’t following instructions.
Being argumentative at work is not a good thing.
I have to stop being so disorganized.
He was fired for being offensive.

3. Reading

You wouldn’t believe the people I work with. I have a colleague who never cleans his desk. He is so sloppy . I have a manager who enjoys being offensive . It’s just awful. And I have a boss who is never in the office. She’s so lazy it’s incredible.
