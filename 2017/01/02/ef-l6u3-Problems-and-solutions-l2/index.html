Problems and solutions L2 Giving your friend advice   2017-01-02


1. Vocabulary

  • ought to
  • had better
  • stressful
A: Sarah - B: MARK
[SARAH] I don’t know why I ever started.
[SARAH] I mean, smoking, makes your hand stink.
[SARAH] Mark’s always thinking about my health.
[SARAH] That’s one reason I decided to come on this trip.
[SARAH] I thought if I could go two weeks, in a car, with Mark, without smoking, I’d definitely break the habit
[SARAH] I should give up smoking.

2. Expressions

sentence
He is very overweight. He ought to lose some weight.
Your health is very poor. You had better quit smoking.
You ought to start exercising more.
You ought to think about working less.
Make sure to drink less coffee.
Be sure to exercise every day.
